M4A1-S Night Terror Wear Pattern Guide



M4A1-S Night Terror

The M4A1-S Night Terror is a restricted skin from the Dreams and Nightmare Collection, which was added to the game on the 21th January 2022.


Float

The M4A1-S Night Terror can be obtained in the float range 0.00 - 0.70. This means the skin exists in all conditions, but can’t have a float higher than 0.70.


Wear Patterns

The M4A1-S Night Terror is wear based. The wear determines the look of the skin itself. For the M4A1-S Night Terror it changes the color of the skin. This means the different colors of the skin depends on the condition of it.


Purple Wear Pattern

The M4A1-S Night Terror changes color to purple. This becomes more visible the worse float the Glock-18 Shinobu has. This means the purple wear pattern is not visible when it’s in factory new condition, but visible when it’s in battle-scarred condition.
