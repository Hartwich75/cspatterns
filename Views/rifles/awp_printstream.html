AWP Printstream Wear Pattern Guide



AWP Printstream

The AWP Printstream is a covert skin from the Fever Collection, which was added to the game on the 31th March 2025.


Float

The AWP Printstream can be obtained in the float range 0.00 - 1.00. This means the skin exists in all conditions.


Wear Patterns

The AWP Printstream is wear based. The wear determines the look of the skin itself. For the AWP Printstream it changes the presence of a word on the skin. This means the different look of the skin comes to life depending on the condition of it.


Lo-|/e” Wear Pattern

The word “Lo-|/e” appears in the empty white box left to the box with a heart inside. This becomes more visible the worse the float the AWP Printstream has. This means “Lo-|/e” is not visible when it’s in factory new condition, but visible when it’s in battle-scarred condition.
