Glock-18 Shinobu Wear Pattern Guide



Glock-18 Shinobu

The Glock-18 Shinobu is a classified skin from the Fever Collection, which was added to the game on the 31th March 2025.




Float

The Glock-18 Shinobu can be obtained in the float range 0.00 - 1.00. This means the skin exists in all conditions.


Wear Patterns

The Glock-18 Shinobu is wear based. The wear determines the look of the skin itself. For the Glock-18 Shinobu it changes the color of the skin. This means the different colors of the skin depends on the condition of it.


Red Wear Pattern

The Glock-18 Shinobu changes from purple to red This becomes more visible the worse float the Glock-18 Shinobu has. This means the red wear pattern is not visible when it’s in factory new condition, but visible when it’s in battle-scarred condition.
