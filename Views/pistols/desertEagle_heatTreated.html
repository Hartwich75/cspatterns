Float

The Desert Eagle Heat Treated can be obtained in the float range 0.00 - 1.00. This means the skin exists in all conditions.


Patterns

Like many other skins in the game, the Desert Eagle Heat Treated is pattern based. The pattern determines the look of the skin itself. For the Desert Eagle Heat Treated it changes the color of the skin, depending on the pattern it can have a different amount of blue and gold on it. The most desired patterns are the ones with the most blue on them.


Blue Gem Patterns

The blue gem patterns are the patterns on the skin where the body of the Desert Eagle Heat Treated contains a certain amount of visible blue. The more visible blue that fills out the body of the Five-Seven the better.
