Die-Cast AK Charm Pattern Guide



Die-Cast AK Charm

The Die-Cast AK Charm is a remarkable collectible from the Small Arms Charm collection, which was added to the game on the 4th October 2024.


Gold Patterns

The low patterns on the charm will make the charm gold. As the pattern gets closer to 1 it will be more gold. This means pattern 1 is the best pattern and the pattern where the charm is as gold as it can get.
