That's Bananas Charm Pattern Guide



That's Bananas Charm

The That's Bananas Charm is a remarkable collectible from the Missing Link Charm collection, which was added to the game on the 4th October 2024.


Brown Patterns

The low patterns on the charm will make the charm brown. As the pattern gets closer to 1 it will be more brown. This means pattern 1 is the best pattern and the pattern where the charm is as brown as it can get.

Inspect in-game

Yellow Patterns

The mid-range patterns on the charm will make the charm yellow. As the pattern gets closer to 50.000 it will be more yellow. This means pattern 50.000 is the best pattern and the pattern where the charm is as yellow as it can get.

Inspect in-game

Green Patterns

The high patterns on the charm will make the charm green. As the pattern gets closer to 100.000 it will be more green. This means pattern 100.000 is the best pattern and the pattern where the charm is as green as it can get.
