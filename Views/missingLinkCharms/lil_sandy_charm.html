Lil' Sandy Charm Pattern Guide



Lil' Sandy Charm

The Lil' Sandy Charm is a remarkable collectible from the Missing Link Charm collection, which was added to the game on the 4th October 2024.


Red Patterns

The low patterns on the charm will make the bathing suit red. As the pattern gets closer to 1 it will be more red. This means pattern 1 is the best pattern and the pattern where the charm is as red as it can get.
