CSPatterns

CSPatterns was created by Hartwich for the community. It's a website to help skin lovers find their perfect match and to help new players get into the skin game.

"I have seen so many people need help for something skin-related in the community and it's not always easy to find the right help. I know this struggle and have therefore decided to make a bunch of guides on skins and charms in CS2. This is where most of my interest in the game lies, and I want the community to be able to find the perfect help for all the many skins out there. This site contains all the guides I have made for the community." - Hartwich.
