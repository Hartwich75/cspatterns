Chicken Lil' Charm Pattern Guide



Chicken Lil' Charm

The Chicken Lil' Charm is a remarkable collectible from the Missing Link Charm collection, which was added to the game on the 4th October 2024.


Patterns

Like any other charm in the game, the Chicken Lil' charm is pattern based. The pattern determines the look of the charm itself. For the Chicken Lil' charm it changes the color of the charm's legs and head, depending on the pattern it can be blue, purple, red, orange, yellow, green, and cyan.


Blue Patterns

The low patterns on the charm will make the charms legs and head blue. As the pattern gets closer to 1 it will be more blue. This means pattern 1 is the best pattern and the pattern where the charm is as blue as it can get.

Blue & Purple Patterns

The patterns around pattern 10.000 on the charm will make the charms legs purple and head blue. As the pattern gets closer to 10.000 the legs will be more purple and the head more blue. This means pattern 10.000 is the best pattern and the pattern where the charms legs/head is as purple/blue as it can get.
