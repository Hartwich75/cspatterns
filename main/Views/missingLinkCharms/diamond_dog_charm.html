Diamond Dog Charm Pattern Guide



Diamond Dog Charm

The Diamond Dog Charm is a exotic collectible from the Missing Link Charm collection, which was added to the game on the 4th October 2024.


Shiny Patterns

The low patterns on the charm will make the charm shiny. As the pattern gets closer to 1 it will be more shiny. This means pattern 1 is the best pattern and the pattern where the charm is as shiny as it can get.

Inspect in-game

Matte Patterns

The high patterns on the charm will make the charm matte and have no shiny. As the pattern gets closer to 100.000 it will be more matte. This means pattern 100.000 is the best pattern and the pattern where the charm is as matte as it can get.

Inspect in-game
