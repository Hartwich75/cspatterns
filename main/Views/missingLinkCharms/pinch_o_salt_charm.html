Pinch O' Salt Charm Pattern Guide



Pinch O' Salt Charm

The Pinch O' Salt Charm is a high grade collectible from the Missing Link Charm collection, which was added to the game on the 4th October 2024.


Patterns

Like any other charm in the game, the Pinch O' Salt charm is pattern based. The pattern determines the look of the charm itself. For the Pinch O' Salt charm it changes the color of the body, depending on the pattern it can be blue, purple, pink, red, yellow, green, or blue.


Dark Blue Patterns

The low patterns on the charm will make the charm be dark blue. As the pattern gets closer to 1 it will be more dark blue. This means pattern 1 is the best pattern and the pattern where the charm is as dark blue as it can get.

Purple Patterns

The low patterns in the range around 10.000 on the charm will make the charm be more purple. As the pattern gets closer to 10.000 it will be more purple. This means pattern 10.000 is the best pattern and the pattern where the charm is as purple as it can get.

Pink Patterns

The semi-low patterns in the range around 30.000 on the charm will make the charm be more pink. As the pattern gets closer to 30.000 it will be more pink. This means pattern 30.000 is the best pattern and the pattern where the charm is as pink as it can get.

Red Patterns

The low-mid patterns on the charm will make the charm red. As the pattern gets closer to 40.000 it will be more red. This means pattern 40.000 is the best pattern and the pattern where the charm is as red as it can get.

Yellow Patterns

The mid-range patterns on the charm will make the charm yellow. As the pattern gets closer to 50.000 it will be more yellow. This means pattern 50.000 is the best pattern and the pattern where the charm is as red as it can get.

Green Patterns

The high-mid patterns on the charm will make the charm yellow. As the pattern gets closer to 80.000 it will be more green. This means pattern 80.000 is the best pattern and the pattern where the charm is as green as it can get.

Light Blue Patterns

The high patterns on the charm will make the charm light blue. As the pattern gets closer to 100.000 it will be more light blue. This means pattern 100.000 is the best pattern and the pattern where the charm is as light blue as it can get.
