Big Kev Charm Pattern Guide



Big Kev Charm

The Big Kev Charm is a high grade collectible from the Missing Link Charm collection, which was added to the game on the 4th October 2024.


Patterns

Like any other charm in the game, the Big Kev charm is pattern based. The pattern determines the look of the charm itself. For the Big Kev charm it changes the color of the shirt and tie, depending on the pattern it can be pink, purple, or blue.


Pink Shirt & Tie Patterns

The low patterns on the charm will make the charms shirt and tie pink. As the pattern gets closer to 1 it will be more pink. This means pattern 1 is the best pattern and the pattern where the charms shirt and tie is as pink as it can get.


Purple Shirt & Tie Patterns

The mid-range patterns on the charm will make the charms shirt and tie purple. As the pattern gets closer to 50.000 it will be more purple. This means pattern 50.000 is the best pattern and the pattern where the charms shirt and tie is as purple as it can get.

Blue Shirt & Tie Patterns

The high patterns on the charm will make the charms shirt and tie blue. As the pattern gets closer to 100.000 it will be more blue. This means pattern 100.000 is the best pattern and the pattern where the charms shirt and tie is as blue as it can get.
