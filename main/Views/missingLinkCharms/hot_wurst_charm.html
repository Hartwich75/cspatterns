Hot Wurst Charm Pattern Guide



Hot Wurst Charm

The Hot Wurst Charm is an extraordinary collectible from the Missing Link Charm collection, which was added to the game on the 4th October 2024.


Patterns

Like any other charm in the game, the Hot Wurst charm is pattern based. The pattern determines the look of the charm itself. For the Hot Wurst charm it changes the rainbow coloring to the fire on the charm, depending on the pattern it can be orange/green or rainbow.


Orange/Green Patterns

The low patterns on the charm will make the fire on the charm orange/green. As the pattern gets closer to 1 it will be more orange/green. This means pattern 1 is the best pattern and the pattern where the charm is as orange/green as it can get.

Rainbow Patterns

The high patterns on the charm will make the fire on the charm rainbow colored. As the pattern gets closer to 100.000 it will be more rainbow colored. This means pattern 100.000 is the best pattern and the pattern where the fire on the charm is as rainbow looking as it can get.
