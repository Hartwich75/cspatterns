Lil' Teacup Charm Pattern Guide



Lil' Teacup Charm

The Lil' Teacup Charm is a remarkable collectible from the Missing Link Charm collection, which was added to the game on the 4th October 2024.


Patterns

Like any other charm in the game, the Lil' Teacup charm is pattern based. The pattern determines the look of the charm itself. For the Lil' Teacup charm it changes the entire look of the charm, depending on the pattern it can have many completely different looks. I have listed images for the patterns: #1, #10.000, #20.000, #30.000, #40.000, #50.000, #60.000, #70.000, #80.000, #90.000, #100.000.

The patterns around pattern 99.500 on the charm will make the charm have the word “smoke” written over its chest. As the pattern gets closer to 99.500 it will be more centered. This means pattern 99.500 is the best pattern and the pattern where “smoke” is written as centered on the chest as it can get.
