Lil' SAS Charm Pattern Guide



Lil' SAS Charm

The Lil' SAS Charm is a high grade collectible from the Missing Link Charm collection, which was added to the game on the 4th October 2024.


Patterns

Like any other charm in the game, the Lil' SAS charm is pattern based. The pattern determines the look of the charm itself. For the Lil' SAS charm it changes the color of the eyes, depending on the pattern it can be red, orange, yellow, green, blue, purple, or pink.


Red Eyes Patterns

The low patterns on the charm will make the charm's eyes red. As the pattern gets closer to 1 it will be more red. This means pattern 1 is the best pattern and the pattern where the charm is as red as it can get.

Orange Eyes Patterns

The low patterns in the range around 10.000 on the charm will make the charm's eyes orange. As the pattern gets closer to 10.000 it will be more orange. This means pattern 10.000 is the best pattern and the pattern where the charm is as orange as it can get.
