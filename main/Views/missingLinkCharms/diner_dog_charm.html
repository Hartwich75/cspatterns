Diner Dog Charm Pattern Guide



Diner Dog Charm

The Diner Dog Charm is a remarkable collectible from the Missing Link Charm collection, which was added to the game on the 4th October 2024.


Patterns

Like any other charm in the game, the Diner Dog charm is pattern based. The pattern determines the look of the charm itself. For the Diner Dog charm it changes the entire look of the charm, depending on the pattern it can have many completely different looks. I have listed images for the patterns: #1, #10.000, #20.000, #30.000, #40.000, #50.000, #60.000, #70.000, #80.000, #90.000, #100.000.

The high patterns on the charm will make the charm heaven eye centered over its chest. As the pattern gets closer to 100.000 it will be more centered. This means pattern 100.000 is the best pattern and the pattern where the eye is placed as centered on the chest as it can get.
