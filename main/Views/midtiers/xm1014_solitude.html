XM1014 Solitude Pattern Guide



XM1014 Solitude

The XM1014 Solitude is a restricted Limited Edition Item skin, which was added to the game on the 31th March 2025. It’s a very popular and unique XM1014 skin. The skin has many different patterns, some more desired than others.


Patterns

Like many other skins in the game, the XM1014 Solitude is pattern based. The pattern determines the look of the skin itself. For the XM1014 Solitude it changes the entire look of the skin, depending on the pattern it can have blue mountains with one sun, blue mountains with two suns, green mountains with one sun, green mountains with two suns, yellow mountains, yellow mountains with one sun, and yellow mountains with two suns.


Blue Mountains and Sun Patterns

The blue mountains and sun patterns are the patterns on the skin where the body of the XM1014 only contains blue mountains and one visible sun. These patterns are put into 3 different tiers. Where tier 1 is the best and tier 3 is the worst.

All the tiers require the pattern to only have full blue mountains on the body.

Tier 1 requires one sun placed centered or around the center of the body and be fully visible or almost fully visible.

Patterns: 33, 105, 135, 225, 717, 815, 948, 930, 984, 997

Tier 2 requires one sun that’s not placed at the center, but is fully visible, or a sun that’s placed at the center, but is not fully visible (at least 50% visibility)

Patterns: 13, 30, 36, 58, 89, 133, 142, 147, 173, 226, 258, 272, 311, 333, 359, 437, 443, 694, 730, 732, 652, 800, 884, 913, 924, 925, 932, 971, 986

Tier 3 requires one sun that’s not placed at the center and not fully visible (at least 50% visibility).
