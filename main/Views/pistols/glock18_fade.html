Glock-18 Fade Pattern Guide



Glock-18 Fade

The Glock-18 Fade is a restricted skin form the Assault collection, which got added to the game on the 14th August 2013 in the arms deal update. Itâ€™s a very expensive and unique skin like the other fade skins.

The Glock-18 Fade has a Fade%, which ranges from 80% - 100% and each unique pattern is connected to the same Fade%. This means pattern 129 will always be the same Fade%. The most desired patterns and Fade% for the Glock-18 Fade are the ones with Max Fade% or Minimum Fade%, usually Max Fade% are the most desired.

The Fade% also has an effect on the colors of the skin itself. Where higher fade will make the skin more purple and less white, and lower fade will make the skin more white and less purple.


Float

The Glock-18 Fade can be obtained in the float range 0.00 - 0.08. This means the skin only exists in Factory New and Minimal Wear condition.
