AUG Amber Fade Pattern Guide



AUG Amber Fade

The AUG Amber Fade is a mil-spec skin form the 2021 Train collection, which got added to the game on the 22th September 2021 as part of the Operation Riptide.

The AUG Amber Fade has a Fade%, which ranges from 80% - 100% and each unique pattern is connected to the same Fade%. This means pattern 129 will always be the same Fade%. The most desired patterns and Fade% for the AUG Amber Fade are the ones with Max Fade% or Minimum Fade%, usually Max Fade% are the most desired.

The Fade% also has an effect on the colors of the skin itself. Where higher fade will make the skin more orange and less green, and lower fade will make the skin more green and less orange.


Float

The AUG Amber Fade can be obtained in the float range 0.00 - 0.40. This means the skin only exists in all conditions except Battle-Scarred. ßIn Well-Worn it can’t have a float higher than 0.40.

The Max Fade% patterns are the patterns being 100% Fade. This means where the skin has the most amount of orange and least amount of green.
