Galil AR Amber Fade Pattern Guide



Galil AR Amber Fade

The Galil AR Amber Fade is a mil-spec skin form the 2021 Dust 2 collection, which got added to the game on the 22th September 2021 as part of the Operation Riptide.

The Galil AR Amber Fade has a Fade%, which ranges from 80% - 100% and each unique pattern is connected to the same Fade%. This means pattern 129 will always be the same Fade%. The most desired patterns and Fade% for the Galil AR Amber Fade are the ones with Max Fade% or Minimum Fade%, usually Max Fade% are the most desired.

The Fade% also has an effect on the colors of the skin itself. Where higher fade will make the skin more orange and less green, and lower fade will make the skin more green and less orange.
