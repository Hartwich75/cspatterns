AWP Fade Pattern Guide



AWP Fade

The AWP Fade is a covert skin form the Control collection, which got added to the game on the 3rd December 2020 during the operation broken fang. Itâ€™s a very expensive and unique skin like the other fade skins.

The AWP Fade has a Fade%, which ranges from 80% - 100% and each unique pattern is connected to the same Fade%. This means pattern 129 will always be the same Fade%. The most desired patterns and Fade% for the AWP Fade are the ones with Max Fade% or Minimum Fade%, usually Max Fade% are the most desired.

The Fade% also has an effect on the colors of the skin itself. Where higher fade will make the skin more yellow and pink, and lower fade will make the skin more purple and pink.


Float

The AWP Fade can be obtained in the float range 0.00 - 0.08. This means the skin only exists in Factory New and Minimal Wear condition.
