AWP Paw Pattern Guide



AWP Paw

The AWP Paw is a restricted skin from the Horizon collection, which got added to the game on the 3rd august 2018. It’s a very popular and affordable AWP skin. The skin has many different patterns, some more desired than others.

The skin itself contains many dogs and cats. The desired patterns on this skin contain some specific dogs, cats and objects. We have the golden cat, golden grenade, golden mask, golden cat with knife, and stoner cat.


Float

The AWP Paw can be obtained in the float range 0.00 - 0.50. This means the skin only exists in all conditions, but in Battle-Scarred condition it can’t have a float higher than 0.50.

The golden cat patterns are the patterns where the golden cat is visible on the skin. It’s more visible on some patterns than others. The golden cat is a golden cat with a red leash and a golden medal around its neck.

The golden cat with knife patterns are the patterns containing the golden cat with a knife. This is not the same golden cat as the one from the golden cat patterns above. Stoner Cat Patterns
