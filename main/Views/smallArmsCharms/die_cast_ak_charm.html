Die-Cast AK Charm Pattern Guide



Die-Cast AK Charm

The Die-Cast AK Charm is a remarkable collectible from the Small Arms Charm collection, which was added to the game on the 4th October 2024.


Patterns

Like any other charm in the game, the Die-Cast AK charm is pattern based. The pattern determines the look of the charm itself. For the Die-Cast AK charm it changes the color of the charm, depending on the pattern it can be gold, cobber, red, pink, purple, dark blue, blue.


Gold Patterns

The low patterns on the charm will make the charm gold. As the pattern gets closer to 1 it will be more gold. This means pattern 1 is the best pattern and the pattern where the charm is as gold as it can get.

Cobber Patterns

The patterns around pattern 10.000 on the charm will make the charm cobber. As the pattern gets closer to 10.000 it will be more cobber. This means pattern 10.000 is the best pattern and the pattern where the charm is as cobber as it can get.
