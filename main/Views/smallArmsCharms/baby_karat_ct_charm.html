Baby Karat CT Charm Pattern Guide



Baby Karat CT Charm

The Baby Karat CT Charm is an extraordinary collectible from the Small Arms Charm collection, which was added to the game on the 4th October 2024.


Rose Gold Patterns

The low patterns on the charm will make the charm rose gold. As the pattern gets closer to 1 it will be more rose gold. This means pattern 1 is the best pattern and the pattern where the charm is as rose gold as it can get.

Cobber Patterns

The mid patterns on the charm will make the charm cobber. As the pattern gets closer to 50.000 it will be more cobber. This means pattern 50.000 is the best pattern and the pattern where the charm is as cobber as it can get.

Gold Patterns

The high patterns on the charm will make the charm gold. As the pattern gets closer to 100.000 it will be more gold. This means pattern 100.000 is the best pattern and the pattern where the charm is as gold as it can get.
