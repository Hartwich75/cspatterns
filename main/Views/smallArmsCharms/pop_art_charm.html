POP Art Charm Pattern Guide



POP Art Charm

The POP Art Charm is a remarkable collectible from the Small Arms Charm collection, which was added to the game on the 4th October 2024.


Patterns

Like any other charm in the game, the POP Art charm is pattern based. The pattern determines the look of the charm itself. For the POP Art charm it changes the color of the POP box, depending on the pattern it can be blue, green, purple, pink, orange, yellow and green. There are many different variations for this charm, and all of them contain 2, 3 or 4 colors at the same time. I have therefore listed images for the patterns: #1, #10.000, #20.000, #30.000, #40.000, #50.000, #60.000, #70.000, #80.000, #90.000, #100.000
