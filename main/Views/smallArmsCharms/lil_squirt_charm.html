Lil' Squirt Charm Pattern Guide



Lil' Squirt Charm

The Lil' Squirt Charm is a exotic collectible from the Small Arms Charm collection, which was added to the game on the 4th October 2024.


Patterns

Like any other charm in the game, the Lil' Squirt charm is pattern based. The pattern determines the look of the charm itself. For the Lil' Squirt charm it changes the color of the charm, depending on the pattern it can be green, blue, purple and pink.


Green Patterns

The low patterns on the charm will make the charm green. As the pattern gets closer to 1 it will be more green. This means pattern 1 is the best pattern and the pattern where the charm is as green as it can get.

Blue Patterns

The mid patterns on the charm will make the charm blue. As the pattern gets closer to 50.000 it will be more blue. This means pattern 50.000 is the best pattern and the pattern where the charm is as blue as it can get.

Purple Patterns

The patterns around pattern 70.000 on the charm will make the charm purple. As the pattern gets closer to 70.000 it will be more purple. This means pattern 70.000 is the best pattern and the pattern where the charm is as purple as it can get.

Pink Patterns

The high patterns on the charm will make the charm pink. As the pattern gets closer to 100.000 it will be more pink. This means pattern 100.000 is the best pattern and the pattern where the charm is as pink as it can get.
