Titeenium AWP Charm Pattern Guide



Titeenium AWP Charm

The Titeenium AWP Charm is a exotic collectible from the Small Arms Charm collection, which was added to the game on the 4th October 2024.


Yellow Patterns

The low patterns on the charm will make the charm yellow. As the pattern gets closer to 1 it will be more yellow. This means pattern 1 is the best pattern and the pattern where the charm is as yellow as it can get.
