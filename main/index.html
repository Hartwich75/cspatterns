What's skin patterns?

Most skins in CS2 is pattern based. This is what makes them unique. Each skin has a pattern index, which is a number that determines how the skin looks. Weapon skins have a pattern index between 0 and 1000, and charms have a pattern index between 0 and 100.000. This means the same skin can look different in one way or the other. Usually, the pattern index changes the color of the entire skin or parts of it. In some cases their can be a little easter egg that shows up on the skin. Therefore some patterns are more desired than others, and can be worth a lot more than market price for that specific skin or charm.
